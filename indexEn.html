Presentation of the band

Original Sin is an alternative rock band formed in 2023. The original duo consisted of Thomas Vankerrebrouck (lead guitar) and Léo Seven (rhythm guitar and vocals). Initially known as Spadz, a Nu-Metal group that faced an issue prompting a change in direction and name. Subsequently, they adopted the name Ice Storm, redirecting their musical style towards a more metal genre with a new vocalist.

After another swift change in style, the group found themselves compelled to rebrand once again, focusing on industrial metal to align with the preferences of new members. The band now goes by the name Krøss. Following a year of formation, Krøss faced a decline and ultimately ended with the members going their separate ways.

In conclusion, Original Sin emerged from the trials faced by Léo and Thom, accompanied by Sebastien Creton, who has been active in the group since the Spadz era as a drummer and later as a keyboardist. Their musical style is now characterized by diverse influences from prominent bands such as Placebo, Depeche Mode, Muse, and Three Days Grace in small doses.

The band focuses on covering popular and non-popular songs, as well as creating original compositions influenced by the ideas and inspirations of the two key members, Léo and Thomas, who have been present since the inception of the prototype group.

Members

Leo Seven A founding member of the initial group, Léo was born on August 7, 1995, in France. Since childhood, he has been devoted to various creative activities, including drawing, writing, inventing, and much later, music. He met Thom in 2018, with whom he would launch a first embryonic group in 2019 where they respectively became rhythm guitarist and lead guitarist.

It was in February 2020 that they together launched their first group under the name Spadz, and the adventure full of twists and turns began.

In the lore of Original Sin, Léo embodies a dual personality: an emotionally troubled man and an inner demon born of numerous sufferings. His lyrics and singing are, from time to time, influenced by this clash of personalities.
